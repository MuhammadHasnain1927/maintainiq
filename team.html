Access control

New team members sign up at the sign-in page and start as Technicians. Promote, demote, or deactivate accounts here — changes take effect on their next page load. Deactivated members are signed out and blocked from every internal page and from all write actions at the database level until reactivated. Administrators can register assets, assign work, and manage the team; Technicians work only on issues assigned to them.
